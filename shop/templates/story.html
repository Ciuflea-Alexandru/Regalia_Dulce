Our Story

Humble Beginnings

In a small chocolate atelier on Ocean Avenue, Jacques and Elodie poured their hearts into crafting exquisite chocolates. They weren't just making sweets; they were sharing a piece of their heritage, meticulously passing down secret traditional recipes and techniques from generation to generation. Each truffle, each bar, was a testament to their unwavering dedication to the art of chocolate.

Jacques Dubois

A passionate French chocolatier who, along with his wife Elodie, founded Regalia Dulce in Carmel-by-the-Sea, California. He brought his culinary dreams across the Atlantic, establishing a legacy of traditional chocolate-making techniques passed down through generations.

Elodie Dubois

Co-founder of Regalia Dulce, Elodie shared Jacques's passion for chocolate and played a vital role in establishing the brand's welcoming atmosphere. Together, they built a beloved institution known for its superior quality artisanal chocolate.
