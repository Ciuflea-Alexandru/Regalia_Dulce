Our Mission

Honoring the Legacy

To preserve and celebrate the rich heritage of Jacques and Elodie Dubois by meticulously crafting chocolates using their time-honored recipes and techniques.

Crafting Excellence

To create artisanal chocolates of superior quality, reflecting our unwavering commitment to the art of chocolate making and delivering a truly exceptional taste experience.

Delighting Generations

To share the sweetness of our tradition with both our cherished local community and new generations of customers, ensuring a warm and inviting atmosphere that embodies the enduring spirit of Regalia Dulce.
